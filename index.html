Cloud deployments can be quite challenging to manage, especially when multiple clouds are involved. Some products help simplify this, such as Terraform, Docker containers, and Kubernetes.

We envisioned that a single interface could be used to deploy to all the cloud providers, with minimal configuration. This idea led to our Hypercompute platform and is currently under development.

The cloud is becoming an increasingly important aspect of enterprise deployments. However, many cloud providers have their APIs and interfaces, making such implementations more difficult. 

Innovate Loop is a startup in the Boston area seeking to simplify that.

Through the use of our Hypercompute platform, customers can deploy to the cloud, across Amazon's AWS, Google Compute, and Microsoft Azure, in a more standardized manner through the use of the Docker platform.

Hypercompute eliminates the need to interact with the providers directly and gives you a consolidated interface and monthly bill.
